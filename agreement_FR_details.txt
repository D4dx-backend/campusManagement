# Functional Requirements (FR) for Madrasa Management Software

## 1. Fee Management
- Define and manage multiple fee categories: Tuition, Transport, Annual Co-curricular, Annual Maintenance, Exam fees, Textbook charges
- Student-wise fee assignment and schedule
- Automated fee calculation and due tracking
- Fee receipt generation and printing
- Fee payment history and outstanding dues tracking
- Concession/discount management

## 2. Payroll Management
- Staff salary structure definition
- Monthly payroll processing
- Allowances, deductions, and advances management
- Payslip generation and printing
- Payroll reports (monthly/yearly)

## 3. Staff Master File
- Staff personal and professional details
- Designation, department, and joining details
- Document uploads (ID, certificates, etc.)
- Active/inactive status management

## 4. Expenditure Management
- Expense category creation
- Expense entry and approval workflow
- Recurring and one-time expense tracking
- Expense receipt upload
- Expense reporting and analytics

## 5. Student Master File
- Student personal, academic, and contact details
- Admission, promotion, and transfer management
- Parent/guardian information
- Document uploads (ID, certificates, etc.)
- Active/inactive status management

## 6. Monthly Income & Expenditure Report
- Automated monthly summary of all income and expenses
- Downloadable and printable reports
- Graphical representation (charts/graphs)

## 7. Monthly Fee Dues & Collection Report
- Student-wise and class-wise fee dues
- Collection summary by date, class, or fee type
- Export to Excel/PDF

## 8. Multiple Users Login & Access Assignment
- Role-based access control (Admin, Accountant, Teacher, etc.)
- User creation, activation, and deactivation
- Permission assignment for modules/features

## 9. Textbook Inventory Management
- Textbook master data (title, author, price, etc.)
- Stock entry, issue, and return tracking
- Inventory alerts (low stock)
- Textbook indent and requisition management

## 10. Transport & Own Transport Report
- Student transport assignment (route, vehicle, driver)
- Transport fee management
- Transport attendance and usage reports
- Own vs. hired transport analysis

## 11. Receipt & Voucher Print
- Customizable receipt and voucher templates
- Print and PDF export options
- Unique numbering and tracking

## 12. Document Conversion to PDF, Email & WhatsApp Option
- Convert receipts, reports, and documents to PDF
- Email and WhatsApp integration for sending documents
- Email/SMS/WhatsApp notification templates

## 13. Cash & Bank Ledger
- Cash and bank account management
- Ledger entry for all transactions
- Reconciliation and reporting

## 14. Expenses Ledger
- Detailed expense ledger by category/date
- Search and filter options
- Export to Excel/PDF

## 15. Income Ledger
- Detailed income ledger by source/date
- Search and filter options
- Export to Excel/PDF

---

This list can be further expanded or customized as per the Client's operational needs.
